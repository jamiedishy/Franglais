What does this mean for English-speakers in Quebec?

With Quebec being the home to a population that is 94% French-speaking, Anglophones have the opportunities to partake in fresh experiences and cultures within this very country. While the adventures are limitless and the unique culture is exhillirating, Anglophones are heavily prevented from contributing to Quebec society due to the language barrier. Whether unsuccessfully securing jobs due to the language barrier, to struggling with ordering dishes at restaurants, Anglophones need to learn French, and they need to learn it fast!
